Samosa Recipie

  • Gather ingredients: maida flour, salt, oil, water, stuffing of your choice (e.g. spiced potato or peas).

  • In a mixing bowl, mix together the maida flour, salt, and oil to form a crumbly mixture.

  • Gradually add water to the mixture and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and set aside for 15 minutes.

  • Meanwhile, prepare the stuffing of your choice by cooking and mashing the ingredients together.

  • Roll out small portions of the dough into thin circles.

  • Spoon the stuffing onto one half of each circle and fold the other half over to form a semi-circle.

  • Press the edges to seal the stuffing inside.

  • Heat oil in a pan and shallow fry the somasas until golden brown on both sides.

  • Serve hot with chutney or sauce of your choice. Enjoy!
